To state that women are better than men is to reduce women to stereotypes typically associated with a motherly role. There is no objective scale of goodness. There are objective scales in a lot of different factors, which can contribute to the understanding of whether one gender is better than another. Women's fertility and ability to produce healthy offspring start declining at a much earlier age. Women sometimes beat men in extreme endurance races. Women have been to space, Mount Everest, the North and South Pole.
