
Alex Jones' conspiracy theories have both positive and negative impacts on society, raising awareness about important issues but also inciting violence. Suppressing his right to speak freely is more damaging than anything he can say, as testing the limits of free speech protects it. However, mainstream media is often biased and can be bought and sold, making it dangerous to suppress alternative forms of media. Jones' influence on his listeners and support for Trump has had a negative impact on society, and his salesmanship influences people through the Infowars store. Political polarization has increased, impacting the ability for both the left and the right to create compromise.
