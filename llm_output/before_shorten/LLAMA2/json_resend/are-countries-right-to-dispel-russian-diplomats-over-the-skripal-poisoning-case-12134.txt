
The debate revolves around whether countries are right to dispel Russian diplomats over the Skripal poisoning case. Arguments in favor of expelling diplomats include the likelihood that Russia was involved in the attack, the need to show solidarity with the UK, and the possibility of provoking Russia and increasing tensions. Arguments against expelling diplomats include the lack of conclusive evidence and the potential for worsening relations between the West and Russia, possibly leading to another war.
