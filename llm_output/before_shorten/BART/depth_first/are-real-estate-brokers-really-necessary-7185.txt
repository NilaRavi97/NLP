Systems like cadastre maintain houses at a certain valuation, so selling by yourself will at least net you the legal price of the house. Using a broker will, most likely, make you gain less than that, because of their commission. Attempting the sale by yourself or hiring a cheap salesperson will yield you a lower price, particularly for higher-value properties. Having an experienced salesperson, who is directly motivated to sell at the highest possible price (Due to the commission they earn) can prove as an advantage to negotiate a better price for the seller. The same value increase can likely be obtained by properly staging your property on your own. In most cases, help is needed to showcase the house due to time constraints from
