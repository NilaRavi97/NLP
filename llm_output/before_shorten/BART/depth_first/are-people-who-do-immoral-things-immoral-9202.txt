People who do immoral things aren't immoral. The definition of Immoral means people who are not meeting societies standards of morality. If you commit an immoral act, you are, by definition, an immoral person. People who are unable to stop themselves from doing bad things must be immoral. People do bad things out of ignorance. This is not something that can be definitively proven; somebody immoral may lie and feign ignorance. Ignorance is not a valid excuse when making moral decisions. Children are imparted with morals from childhood, so ignorance is a poor excuse for moral deficits.
