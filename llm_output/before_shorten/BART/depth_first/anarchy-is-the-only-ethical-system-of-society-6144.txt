Anarchy is the only ethical system of society. Humans are naturally ethical and anarchy provides them with the freedom to live ethically. Natural ethics is a contradiction. Ethics doesn't follow from the laws of nature. It is a construct of sentient actors (humans), who decide what is ethical and what is not. For the last few millenia human morals evolved in different legal systems. Claiming that human morals are natural and not a product of thousands of years of operation within these systems is unsupported. The selection of all such ideals or standards, while not arbitrary, is nevertheless subjective. There exists no objective standard against which to measure what is most or least ethical.
