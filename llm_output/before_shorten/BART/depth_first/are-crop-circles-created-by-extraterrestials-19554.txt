Are crop circles made by extraterrestrials? There is no doubt that crop circles are made by humans. Most of them look like something that a bored logo designer would do. But there are a handful that are too large and intricate to have been done in this fashion. Many have no foot prints leading to and from them. Most are to complex and large to be done at night with wooden boards. Some crop circles have the plant's stalk bent but not broken. This proves that they're exclusively the product of humans. If humans were the creators they simply would not be able to keep all of the plants intact. There is clearly a bigger reason for the circles. There has been slight detection of radiation and other phenomenon at crop circles
