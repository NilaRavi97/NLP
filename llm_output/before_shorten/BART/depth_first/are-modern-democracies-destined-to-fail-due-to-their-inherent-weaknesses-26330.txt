Modern democracies are destined to fail due to their inherent weaknesses. Democracy served its purpose as the best political system for the socio-economic conditions. As those change, so it becomes unable to meet the needs of those who hold power. To avoid failure democracies must adapt and at least seem to serve most citizens. Democracy's naturally slow pace can get in the way of rapid response when it seems to be called for (eg: regulating rapidly advancing science and technology, combating climate change). Democracy emerged in societies where a majority of the population had the means and opportunity to make their voices heard.
