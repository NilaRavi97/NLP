Cryptocurrencies, such as Bitcoin and others, are beneficial for the world. They are the natural progression of what money is becoming in the digital age. Less than 10% of the world's currency exists in coins and paper money - most of it is purely virtual. The strength in digital currency lies in its universal character. Every person can use it in every country. It unites the world economically. The Euro did not really do a very good job at uniting consistent and widespread use as a common currency. It also allows for the purchase of illegal things that people are ashamed of. The act of buying legal, but shameful, items/services is not the biggest risk of detection - the delivery or use is.
