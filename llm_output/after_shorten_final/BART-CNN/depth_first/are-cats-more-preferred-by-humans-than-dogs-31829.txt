Cats have undergone fewer genetic changes than dogs in the process of "domestication" This implies that dogs are more tailored to our preferences than cats can be. Dogs love their humans five times more than cats. Cats are typically less expensive than dogs to care for. Boarding costs for cats are less than most dogs due to their small size. Cat allergies are more prevalent than allergies to dogs. The top 10 cat-owning countries in the world have an estimated 204 million felines; in comparison, the top 10 dog- owning countries have fewer than 173 million canines.
