There is no scientific proof that ghosts are real. Claims of ghost sightings are based on personal anecdote. Mirror neurons, a common player in sleep paralysis, may to blame for seeing ghosts. Your brain hallucinates your conscious reality. Most of the video "evidence" are hoaxes. There are accounts of people trying to contact ghosts that never receive any evidence. Harry Houdini made a promise to his wife that, if there was any possible way to communicate with her following his death, he would find it.
