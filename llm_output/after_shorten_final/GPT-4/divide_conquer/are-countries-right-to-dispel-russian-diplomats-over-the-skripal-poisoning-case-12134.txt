Countries are expelling Russian diplomats in solidarity with the UK, which blames Russia for the Skripal poisoning, challenging Putin's alleged strategy to divide the West. Critics argue the punitive response lacks conclusive evidence, pointing out the UK's delayed consultation with the OPCW and failure to give Russia a sample of the nerve agent. President Trump's avoidance of the issue with Putin raises fears of escalating tensions leading to war, underlining the unease some feel about prioritizing Western unity over individual national interests.
