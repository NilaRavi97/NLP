In the debate, shower gel is prized for hygiene in shared spaces but faulted for its environmental toll from plastic packaging and transport inefficiency. Bar soap, seen as convenient and eco-friendlier due to minimal packaging, is also touted for its cost-effectiveness and longevity. Conversely, bar soap is deemed less suitable for hair washing and is as eye-irritating as gel. Its tendency to be dropped is noted but is a trifling drawback amidst its advantages.
