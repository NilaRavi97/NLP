
In a democracy, a free press is crucial for exposing corruption, holding leaders accountable, and upholding norms. High-quality reporting provides citizens with accurate information, despite clickbait content. A free press supports citizens' right to criticize and hold politicians accountable, leading to politicians who reflect voters' values. Media controlled by politicians may prioritize self-interest over accountability. While freedom of speech is a democratic value, harmful speech can harm society. Ultimately, published opinions reflect owners' interests, not the people's. (90 words)
