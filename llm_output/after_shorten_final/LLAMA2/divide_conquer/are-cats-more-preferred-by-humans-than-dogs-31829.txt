
Dogs and cats have different traits, with dogs having more brain cells, indicating intelligence. Dogs form stronger bonds with humans, while cats are seen as independent. Some dog breeds are better at survival than cats, but most people prefer dogs, with 63% of pet owners owning dogs and 43% owning cats. Dogs show more affection towards humans, with higher oxytocin levels during playtime, while cats express affection but humans often misinterpret their signs. Cats are generally cheaper to care for than dogs due to their size.
