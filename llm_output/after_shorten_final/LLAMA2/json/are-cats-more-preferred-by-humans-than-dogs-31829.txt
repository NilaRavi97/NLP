
The debate revolves around whether cats or dogs are better pets. The 'Pro' arguments for cats include their self-sufficiency, street smarts, and lower cost of care. The 'Pro' arguments for dogs include their loyalty, affection, and higher open attributes such as happiness. The 'Con' arguments against cats include their independence and aloofness, while the 'Con' arguments against dogs include their need for more attention and higher cost of care.
